Note from Ghost

Dear, dear reader, you've made a terrible mistake.
You must have known of stories of me, the Library Ghost,
who murders all that stay past midnight.

Now, now, the doors are all locked,
and you are at my whim and mercy.
Your fault, completely, that you stayed past closing time.

I am an old soul,
Malicious but bored,
So you are given a chance of escape.
Follow my clues, play my games,
to find the secret way out.
May the cleverest survive.

To help your chances,
For the first challenge,
Here is the Morse Code chart.
