You have a special, secret place in the library that
nobody ever goes to. You go there and accidently fall
asleep there. When you wake up, it is already 11 pm,
an hour after the library has closed. Rumors has it
that this library is haunted, and anyone who has
stayed past midnight was found dead in the morning.
Presumably killed by the same ghost that moves books
around and makes creepy noises at night. You have until
midnight to get out.
